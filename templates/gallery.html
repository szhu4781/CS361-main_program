Click on the arrows on the sides of the image slider to view the previous or next image.

To view an image at full size, simply click on the current image being displayed and it will display the full-sized image along with unique buttons.

The image attributes will be displayed when you click on the Image Info button located at the bottom of the full-size image.

Clicking on the Rate button will display a rating form prompting for a rating from 1 to 5. Click on the button again to hide the form.

To close out of full-size view, click the X at the top-right in the modal, or click anywhere on your screen aside from the image.
